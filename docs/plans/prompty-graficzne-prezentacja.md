# 🎨 Prompty Graficzne do Prezentacji: "Playwright dla niecierpliwych"

Ten plik zawiera profesjonalne prompty do generowania grafik (Gemini, DALL-E 3, Midjourney). 
Styl: **Cinematic Minimalist Tech** (idealny do ciemnego motywu prezentacji).

---

## 🖼️ Slajd 0: Tytuł (Inspiracja i Technologia)
**Prompt:**
> "A cinematic close-up of a futuristic, sleek white robot hand gently touching a translucent glass screen with glowing digital code. Neon blue and soft teal lighting, dark minimalist background, 8k resolution, photorealistic, high-tech aesthetic, designed for a dark mode presentation."

---

## 🖼️ Slajd 1: Moja Historia (Rozwój i Droga)
**Prompt:**
> "A stylized 3D illustration of a journey path glowing in gold, starting from a small simple cube and leading to a complex crystalline structure. Dark charcoal background, soft bokeh lights, minimalist, clean composition, representing growth and evolution."

---

## 🖼️ Slajd 2: Krzywa Nauki (Uproszczenie)
**Prompt:**
> "A conceptual 3D render of a steep, dark mountain range being sliced perfectly flat by a bright laser beam of light, creating a smooth horizontal highway. Minimalist isometric style, deep blue and white colors, representing the simplification of complex tasks, high contrast."

---

## 🖼️ Slajd 3: Case Study (Analiza i Biznes)
**Prompt:**
> "A futuristic digital magnifying glass scanning a 3D holographic city map. Certain buildings are highlighted with glowing amber light beams. Sophisticated UI elements floating in the air, dark tech laboratory atmosphere, cinematic lighting, 8k."

---

## 🖼️ Slajd 4: Co mnie zaskoczyło (Magia i Time Travel)
**Prompt:**
> "An artistic representation of 'Time Travel' in technology: multiple translucent layers of a web browser overlapping each other, showing different stages of a process. Soft glowing edges, emerald green and electric blue accents, abstract digital art, minimalist."

---

## 🖼️ Slajd 5: Mentor AI (Architektura i Nauka)
**Prompt:**
> "A glowing, translucent human brain silhouette made of golden neural networks, connected to a perfectly organized 3D grid of architectural blueprints. Minimalist, intellectual, dark background with soft teal highlights, representing AI-assisted learning."

---

## 🖼️ Slajd 6: Przyszłość (Agentic Workflow / MCP)
**Prompt:**
> "A futuristic command center interface showing a 'Mission Control' for AI agents. Floating holographic icons representing different web tools, a robotic eye at the center, soft violet and blue glow, hyper-realistic, wide angle shot, cinematic."

---

## 🖼️ Slajd 9: Podsumowanie (Nowy Dzień / Nadzieja)
**Prompt:**
> "A hopeful, minimalist horizon at dawn with a glowing digital sun rising over a landscape of clean code lines. Dramatic shadows, warm gold and deep navy blue color palette, representing a new era of software engineering, 8k."

---

### 💡 Porady Techniczne:
1. **Format:** Jeśli generator na to pozwala, wybierz proporcje **16:9** (panoramiczne).
2. **Spójność:** Wszystkie prompty zawierają frazę "minimalist", co pomoże zachować spójny wygląd całej prezentacji.
3. **Edycja:** Jeśli obraz jest zbyt jasny, dodaj: *"low-key lighting, deep blacks, high contrast"*.
